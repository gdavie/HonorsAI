Detailed Description

This is the Slotted PLGP method. The idea is to treat each factor in a Blueprint as a 'slot' which we can interchange factors in and out of until we get the best blueprint.

Iterates through the subpopulations and performs evolutionary operations on them in turn. Once a subpop has evolved, the blueprints are iterated through and pointers to the correct factors in each subpopulation are updated.

A set has been introduced to catpure the indices of the factors that compose each blueprint. Get and set methods were necessitated by the inheritance structure from ipopulation, possible optimisation steps here.
